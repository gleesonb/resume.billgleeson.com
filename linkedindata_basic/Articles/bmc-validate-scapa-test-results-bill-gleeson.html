BMC validate Scapa test results

Created on 2015-10-27 15:45

Published on 2015-10-27 15:57

We posted a paper a few months ago on some side by side testing that we did with our colleagues at Alderstone Consulting - we performed side by side testing of BMC Remedy version 7.6, 8.1 and 9.0. Having identified a performance slowdown on testing 9.0 out of the box, BMC Software took an interest and after some discussion took Scapa TPP internally to repeat the tests.

Their results showed exactly the same performance signature that we had uncovered. Working with their own teams, they retested against a tuned Remedy system - the Remedy tuning parameters listed in this post improved 9.0 performance.

We like to thank BMC and Alderstone for their cooperation in this exercise, which we really believe shows the value of performance testing systems, even out of the box ones!
